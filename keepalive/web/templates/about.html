About GoKeepalive

GoKeepalive v0.1.0

I'll keep your content alive... forever~ â™¡

GoKeepalive is a content reinsertion tool for Hyphanet/Freenet. It monitors your freesites and automatically reinserts blocks that are becoming unavailable, ensuring your content stays accessible on the network.

Think of it as a devoted guardian that watches over your content 24/7, never letting it fade away into the void of forgotten data~

Features

  • Automatic availability monitoring
  • Smart sample-based testing
  • Concurrent block reinsertion
  • Real-time progress tracking
  • Configurable thresholds
  • Built-in web interface
